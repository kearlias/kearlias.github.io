About

My name is James Hagen. I Studied at NSCCs' Ivany campus as an electronics engineer and graduated in 2020. I have always enjoyed building and repairing things and have had an aptitude for math, so electronics repair and development just made sense.

Working as a computer repair technician helped to expand my interest in electronics. Specifically working with LED kits and liquid cool setups made me realise that so many different types of electronics can be brought together to create amazing products.
